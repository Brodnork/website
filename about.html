About

Hi there! I'm Bee! I'm an artist, animator, and game developer from Canada who likes telling uplifing stories and drawing cute characters and creatures. While most of my work is fairly lighthearted, I enjoy creating horror projects from time to time too! I'm also the head of Frogteam Games, a little team I formed while working on Frogsong.

This is my personal website! I created it to be a sort of portfolio and hub for my various projects and work.
