About

Hi there! I'm Brandon Braun, but most of my friends call me Bee. I'm an artist, animator, and game developer from Canada who likes telling uplifing stories drawing cute characters. While most of my work is fairly lighthearted, I do enjoy creating horror projects from time to time. I'm also the head of Frogteam Games, a little team I formed while working on Frogsong, my first commercial game!

This is my personal website! I created it to be a sort of portfolio and hub for my various projects and work.
