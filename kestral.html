The Kestral: An Ancient World

The Kestral: An Ancient World was a Minecraft mod I started working on back in 2020 that adds a desolate and lonely new dimension to explore full of strange and terrifying creatures. The project was inspired by the feelings that beta Minecraft gave me, exploring an empty and sometimes unnerving world.

I haven't worked on it in quite a while, but would like to pick it back up again some day.
